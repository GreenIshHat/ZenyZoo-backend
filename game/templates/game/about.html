About ZenyZoo (Alpha Release)

ZenyZoo is a turn-based card-battle MVP built with Django, DRF & Channels. We’re alpha—expect breaking changes as we iterate!

Credits

  • Game design & development: GreenIshHat
  • Card art generation: Custom scripts + DALL·E
  • Physical card printing: makeplayingcards.com
  • Music & SFX: Ove Melaa (Omsofware@hotmail.com)
  • Web framework: Django, Django REST Framework, Django Channels

Database & Data

In production we’re on Neon Postgres. During alpha, accounts & match data may be reset without notice.
